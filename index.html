How Would This Work for Genia Foods?

🛒 Self-Serve: Buyers Order Directly

Your wholesale customers log in, see their personalized catalog with negotiated pricing, and place orders 24/7.

  • Buyer logs in with their company credentials
  • Sees only products available in their region (Mexico or Peru source)
  • Customer-specific pricing loads automatically
  • Adds avocados, citrus, and other produce to cart
  • Volume discounts applied at checkout
  • Pays with saved ACH or requests Net 30 terms
  • Order flows to your ERP/Sentinel for fulfillment

📞 Sales-Assisted: Reps Enter Orders

For customers who prefer phone ordering, your sales team uses the admin to place orders on their behalf.

  • Sales rep receives call from restaurant buyer
  • Opens Shopify admin, selects the customer's company
  • Creates draft order with requested items
  • Customer-specific pricing and terms auto-apply
  • Sends invoice or processes payment
  • Order syncs to fulfillment systems
  • Customer receives confirmation email

🔄 Hybrid: Best of Both Worlds

Offer self-serve for reorders and simple purchases, with sales team support for complex orders or new customers.

  • New customers submit account request form
  • Your team reviews and approves with appropriate pricing tier
  • Customer enabled for self-serve ordering
  • Reorders happen automatically, 24/7
  • Complex orders or special requests go through sales
  • All orders in one system regardless of channel
  • Full visibility for your team and customers
